Pipeline Puzzle

Puzzle, as its name indicates, is a puzzle game in which you will find a board on which there are some pieces or pipes that you can rotate by clicking on them. The objective of the game is to rotate these pipes to create a path from the origin to the end of the pipe and thus be able to overcome the level.

Notes Transponder

This application basically consists of changing a series of notes that you enter by clicking on the piano notes and then you choose the number of positive or negative semitones to which you want the notes that you previously entered to be transposed. This is useful for instruments that have their notes transposed naturally like the saxophone or also for lowering or raising the pitch of a song.
